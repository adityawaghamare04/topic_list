About

Subject-Match is an online platform designed to address educational disparities by providing personalized tutoring services. Our mission is to connect students with experienced tutors who can help them excel in their academic endeavors.

How It Works

  1. Sign up for an account.
  2. Select your preferred subjects.
  3. Receive personalized tutor recommendations based on your academic performance and subject preferences.
  4. Schedule sessions with your chosen tutor and start learning!

Benefits

  • Personalized learning tailored to individual needs.
  • Access to experienced tutors in various subjects.
  • Flexible scheduling to accommodate busy lifestyles.
  • Continuous support and guidance to improve academic performance.
